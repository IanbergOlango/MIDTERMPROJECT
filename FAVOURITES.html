My Favourites

You might find some of it interesting, so consider it as my recommendations as well.

MOVIE: La La Land

I love this movie so much because this movie is very well made. From the songs, scripts, to cinematography. Plus it is also the favourite film of my favourite artist Dodie Clark.

ANIME: Your Lie in April

This Anime is sad but I love this so much. It conveys a message that life must go on cause the world waits for no one. It also has piano/violin performances so it entertained me because I like and appreciate music too.

BAND: Paramore

Back when I was a kid my older sister always listens to them, that's how I found out about them. As I grew older I found myself listening to their music so much and then I realized I love this band! I have been following them through their music journey since then.

LOCAL ARTIST: Arthur Nery

It was Kagay-An Festival Live Band that I found out about Arthur Nery. His Stage presence was really good and so was his voice. After that event, I became a huge fan of him and his music. Specially when I found out that he is from Cagayan de Oro too.

SERIES: Stranger Things

I found out about this series from a friend who suggested it to me. It was way back around the airing of Season1. Until then, I followed this series with my friends. It's really wholesome and fun to watch.

POET: Lang Leav

I found Lang Leav on Pinterest. I was following another poet named Rupi Kaur that time and since I was scrolling through poetry section, Pinterest decided to recommend her works to me. I bought some of her books and finished each in one sitting because I can't put it down.

POETRY BOOK: Lullabies - Lang L.

Of all the poetry books Lang Leav wrote that I have read, Lullabies was the best for me because it is subtle. It has poems about Love and Romance but she balanced it with Tragedy as well.

ARTIST: Dodie Clark

This artist that I love so much, I found on YouTube. I have been watching Jon Cozarts After Ever After Series. Apparently He and Dodie made a lot of collaborations so I browsed through her channel and fell inlove with her works.

BOOK: Paper Towns

There has been people raving about Paper Towns so much so I decided to read the book. I enjoyed it so much cause it envolves puzzle and mystery. The plot was great and so are the lines. It tells it's readers to live the life you want to live.

MANGA: Fairy Tail

I decided to read this manga because way back in junior high school, I bought plenty of Fairytyail posters even though I dont know the whole story, and wanted to make up for it. I catched up to it and even though there were cringy parts of it, I still enjoyed it.

MANHUA: ReLIFE

This manhua was recommended by the same person who recommended Stranger Things. The story conveys a message that even if you fail, there would still be a chance to get back on your feet and start again. The plot was interesting and funny so I enjoyed it as well.

SONG: Anyway - Tori Kelly

I have always been a fan of Tori Kelly after her collab with Aj Rafael and followed her eversince. And then she released her Unbreakable Smile album that has Anyway in it. I love the song so much cause it is saying that "Love me or Hate me, I'm still gonna be me".
